My Story




I graduated from university with a Bachelor’s degree in Mass Communications and worked for over a decade as a copywriter. My career took me on some exciting adventures, from touring cruise ships to covering food festivals. I even worked at Australia’s biggest food magazine, creating content and cooking alongside talented chefs, food stylists and food photographers. A few years into my career, I enrolled in graphic design classes at night (which I loved). But two years later with my Diploma in Graphic Design in hand, I felt like I had nowhere to channel my newfound skills.

Obsessed with baking and food blogs, I decided to join the fun and tentatively created Sweetest Menu in 2014. I started out posting recipes here and there in my spare time. I quickly fell in love with the process of testing recipes, taking photos and writing endlessly about food. I was working on Sweetest Menu before work, after work and on the weekends. Finally after six years as a side hustle, and five months pregnant with my daughter, I went full time with Sweetest Menu in March 2020.

A little more about sweetest menu
Sweetest Menu may have had humble beginnings, but it’s now a popular baking resource for bakers all around the world. It’s a place where we can celebrate the joy of baking each and every day.
Each recipe is designed to produce delicious desserts that work, are easy to follow and produce amazing results for minimum fuss. My recipes have been published in Taste, Australia’s top-selling food magazine, and on taste.com.au. Sweetest Menu recipes have also been featured on popular lifestyle sites including PopSugar, Brit & Co, BuzzFeed, Huffington Post and HOORAY Magazine. I’ve also had the privilege of working with amazing food brands including Kitchen Aid, Williams and Sonoma and Almond Breeze.
